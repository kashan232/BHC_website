Benazir Hari Card

Initiative of Government of Sindh

The Agriculture Department, Government of Sindh has launched the Benazir Hari Card, a transformative initiative designed to empower small farmers across the province. This card offers access to vital resources, including subsidies on seeds, fertilizers, and interest-free loans, with a goal of registering 1.4 million farmers across Sindh. The Benazir Hari Card aims to enhance agricultural productivity and contribute significantly to the economic development of Sindh and Pakistan. By ensuring accurate data and providing accessible financial options, such as bank accounts, ATM cards, biometric identification, and beneficiary verification, this program marks a new era of agricultural support, promoting growth and sustainability in the farming sector.
